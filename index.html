My Summer Vacation

Introduction

My name is Lily Lyons and I am a senior at Northview High School. During my summer vacation, I worked at Goodwill, played video games, and went to the library.

Goodwill

From June 3 to July 3, I worked at Goodwill. My supervisor's name was Jordan and my coworker's name was Lyndon. There were many tasks that we had to do, like sorting hangers, cleaning shelves, and pushing carts. I earned almost $1,000 doing this job.

Video Games

During the summer, I also played a lot of video games. Since my old Nintendo Switch broke, I used the money I earned from my job to buy a new one. I also bought some Dragon Quest and Final Fantasy games to play.

Library

On August 8, I went to the library with my grandma. I got my very first library card there, so now I can check out my own books. I checked out four crocheting books that day.

Conclusion

These experiences mean that I am going to be an adult and am capable of taking care of myself.
